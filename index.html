FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining Shiksha Mantra.

How do I book a free demo class?+
Simply click the "Book Free Demo Class" button, fill in your details and preferred subject, and our team will confirm a slot within 24 hours.
What boards do you cover?+
We cover CBSE, ICSE, and UP Board for Classes 1–12. Our curriculum is designed to align with all major board examination patterns.
What is the batch size?+
We keep batch sizes small (8–15 students) to ensure every student gets personalised attention from the teacher.
Are IT programs suitable for beginners?+
Yes! Our Python, Web Development, and Java courses start from absolute basics. No prior coding experience is required.
Do you provide study material?+
Yes, all enrolled students receive comprehensive printed notes, practice sheets, and access to digital resources for their respective programmes.
Can I switch batch timing after enrollment?+
Absolutely. Subject to availability, students can change their batch timing. Just contact our front desk or WhatsApp us.
